Welcome to Mugs & Kisses! We started in 2018 as a small coffee bar. We love coffee (and tea!), and wanted to share our passion. One year later we started sourcing fair-trade beans from trusted farmers and roasters to make even better, more sustainable brews. We believe there is a perfect blend for each person - come and find out which one is yours!
