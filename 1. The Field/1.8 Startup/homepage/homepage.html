OUR STORY

The name "Mugs & Kisses" comes from a funny misspelled text that one of our friends sent us a few years ago.
We started in 2018 as a small café. We love coffee (and tea!), and wanted to share our passion. One year later we started sourcing fair-trade beans from trusted farmers and roasters to make even better, more sustainable brews.

OUR CAFÉ

Coffee (obviously), but also tea, chocolate, plant-based milk... We believe there is a perfect blend for each person - come and find out which one is yours! Just have a look at our menu.

OUR COFFEE

Interested in trying our coffee at home or at work? You can buy a bag of whole coffee beans and try your hand at grinding! It's fun, we promise. Check our wholesale section and contact us for more information.

OUR EVENTS

Interested in a short barista traning? Would you like to come and socialize at our board games afternoons? Or maybe stand-up is more your thing? Check out our events and come join the fun!
